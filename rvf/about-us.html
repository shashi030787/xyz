OUR MISSION

Raising Voice foundation works to bring about change in the lives of children,young people,old people and vulnerable individuals. Our mission to bring hope to those with none and change the lives of everyone we work with, including our staff, volunteers, donors and partners.

OUR STORY

The Raising Voice Foundation (RVF) was established in the year of 2017 by Smt. Moumita Das, with aim to help those people who really need our support. "RVF" works with some most disadvanteg "Children and Women" in the world today. It is a Non-Governmental Organisation.

How to become a volunteer

    Applicants are request to submit the following by mail.
  • A detailed C.V/ Resume.
  • Reference letter from the college if student
  • On receipt of the above documents .RVF may arrange telephonic or face to face interaction with the applicant.
  • If selected RVF will issue a letter of offer to the internship to sign
  • Minimum period of placement is 4 weeks.
  • Internship will be given a RVF certificate upon completion of their internship RVf does not provide any stipend or re-imbursement to intern.
  • The intern shall respect and commit them towards the goal and objective of RVF.
  • The intern shall be courteous, friendly and cooperative.
  • If the intern is not able to accomplish the responsibility it should be informed advance to RVF mentor.
  • The internship can be terminated in any incident of misconduct, non-performance or indiscipline by the RVF mentor.
