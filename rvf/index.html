About Us

WHO WE ARE

The Raising Voice Foundation (RVF) was established in the year of 2017 by Smt. Moumita Das, with aim to help those people who really need our support. "RVF" works with some most disadvantage "Children and Women" in the world today. It is a Non-Governmental Organisation.

Our Mission

Raising Voice foundation works to bring about change in the lives of children,young people,old people and vulnerable individuals. Our mission to bring hope to those with none and change the lives of everyone we work with, including our staff, volunteers, donors and partners.

Our Vision

Our Aim to improve socity, bettering the communities they serve, and provide to food, home and health check-up for poor people.

Our Services

"22nd December 2017" Health check-up camp at "Hooghly" With "AMRI hospital" From “Raising Voice Foundation"

“11th March 2018” Cancer Awareness camp at "Hooghly" for Womens From “Raising Voice Foundation”

Our Work

We do following these Services

  • Cancer Awareness.
  • General Health Awareness.
  • Provide to food for poor people.
  • Medical Treatment.
  • Domestic Violence for Women.
  • Provide home, food and medical treatment for old people.
  • Camp Celebration with poor child.
  • Nursing Training.
